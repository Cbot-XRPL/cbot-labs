XRPL Trading Strategies — Watch & Simulate

Owner-only monitoring page. No seeds or signing performed. This is a read-only strategy dashboard that (a) enumerates live strategy definitions, (b) simulates indicator signals locally for demo/observability, and (c) shows prerequisites for real execution (trustlines, min balances, risk controls).

Simulation uses a local RSI oscillator demo to produce signal flashes for each strategy. To actually run strategies, wire server-side XRPL adapter and add owner-only execution endpoints (seeds must be stored in secrets manager).

Operational checklist

- Ensure OWNER_WALLETS includes your XRPL address(es).
- Add required trustlines (example: rUSD gateway) from the owner wallet before attempting to hold issued tokens.
- Keep signing/seeds out of repo: set XRPL_WALLET_SEED in your runtime secrets manager only.
- Use server-side adapters in lib/xrpl/ to perform sensitive actions; the frontend is read-only.
